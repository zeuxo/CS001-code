Archipelago

The Heart of Southeast Asia on Every Plate

Archipelago was born
from the collective vision
of Mejia, Brioso, Urbina,
and Vargas — four chefs
united by friendship and
a shared reverence for
Southeast Asia's artistry
and soul. Drawing from
decades of experience in
the region's most
respected kitchens, they
bring the discipline,
creativity, and cultural
depth that define the
Southeast Asian spirit

Guided by tradition yet
driven by innovation,
Archipelago is space where
craftsmanship transcends
borders. Every element —
from the sourcing of
ingredients to the
presentation of each dish —
reflects a balance of
authenticity and refinement.

MEET THE
Jasmine
Brioso

Chef Brioso found her
rhythm in the vibrant pulse
of Thailand and Vietnam —
where flavor is both
discipline and chaos,
harmony and rebellion. Her
philosophy is rooted in
emotion: she cooks to
remember, but also to
surprise. Every dish she
touches seeks balance not in
perfection, but in feeling —
the satisfaction of spice
meeting calm.

Charles
Mejia

Chef Mejia grew up among the
tides and hearts of the
Philippines, where every
meal is a story and every
ingredient had memory. His
cooking draws from that
intimacy — the tenderness of
family recipes refined into
new forms. To him, a dish is a
bridge between past and
present, between the
warmth of tradition and the
clarity of modern craft.

Patricia
Vargas

Chef Vargas stands at the
crossroads of Malaysia and
Singapore — cultures woven
together by trade, taste, and
time. She sees cuisine as
conversation: tradition
meeting progress, mastery
meeting humility. Her
creations are refined yet
deeply personal, built from
precision and the belief that
elegance begin with
understanding.

Joruss
Urbina

Chef Urbina carries the soul
of Indonesia and Brunei—
vast, layered, and unafraid of
contrast. His hands move like
those of an artisan, guided by
memory and intuition. In his
kitchen, time slows; smoke,
spice, and patience converge
into something almost
sacred. Urbina's food speaks
softly but lingers, much like
the islands that inspire him.

Together, they form the foundation of Archipelago: four voices,
one vision. Each brings a distinct rhythm to the table, composing
a collective narrative that honors the region's soul while shaping
its future

MISSION AND VISION

At Archipelago, our mission is to honor and elevate the culinary
heritage of Southeast Asia through craftsmanship, collaboration,
and deep respect for tradition. Guided by the belief that cuisine
transcends borders, we envision Archipelago as a living symbol
of Southeast Asia's unity in diversity — a space where flavors,
artistry, and culture converge to inspire a global appreciation for
the region's enduring soul.
